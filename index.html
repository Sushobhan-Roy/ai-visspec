Project

Hardware

  • RGB LED: Emits visible light spectrum
  • LDR: Measures reflected light intensity
  • ESP32: Microcontroller for data acquisition

AI Integration

A Python-based AI model processes the RGB data to predict medicine authenticity. It classifies samples as Genuine or Fake.

Aim

  • Ensure medicine integrity and patient safety
  • Leverage spectroscopy + AI for real-time detection
  • Affordable, portable solution for widespread use
  • Raise awareness about counterfeit medicine risks
